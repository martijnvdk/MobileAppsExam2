MyCV Help
Welcome to my CV app! The goal of this app is to showcase my CV in digital form. In the side menu of this page, you can navigate to different sections of the app. The 'Home' button will bring you to the front page of my CV. From there, the tabs will guide you to the other sections.

The 'Sign out' button will log the user off, and will be unable to view the main portion of the app until they log in again. New users can register using the registration form.

This app was created by Martijn van de Kamer for the course R0334-3002 Introduction to Mobile App Design and Development at Laurea University of Applied Sciences. It's been very educational and fun building this, and I hope you'll have a good experience! I will be happy to answer questions, should you have any.
