MyCV Help
Instructions The goal of this app is to showcase my CV in digital form. In the side menu of this page, you can navigate to different sections of the app. The 'Home' button will bring you to the front page of my CV. From there, the tabs will guide you to the other sections.

The 'Sign out' button will log the mock user off, and will be unable to view the main portion of the app until they log in again. Currently, there's only one username and password working, so also the registration page does not function fully.
